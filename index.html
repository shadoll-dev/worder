How to Play

Guess the 5-letter word in 6 tries.

Each guess must be a valid 5-letter word. Press Enter to submit.

After each guess, the color of the tiles will change:

  • W Correct letter, correct spot
  • O Correct letter, wrong spot
  • X Letter not in the word
